ABOUT

All ECE majors are required to take a course called Car Lab where groups of 2 build an autonomous electric line-reader car. I took it last semester and my car lab partner and I were talking about how it'd be good practice to build something new every week/month.

When I was looking for inspiration on what to build during the break, I found it difficult to compile videos and images for my projects in a way that looked nice. A website that does this could be a solution. Since I had this problem, I imagine my car lab partner probably also experienced something similar so here's a website for her with a compilation of potential projects. - YL
